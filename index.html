Best Overall Budget Cameras

Best Overall point-and-shoot Camera

A budget start to photography may include the Canon PowerShot SX410 IS. You can get this camera for
$140. This may not be the cheapest point-and-shoot camera because you can get much better than the
cheapest for only a bit of extra money and that bit of extra money can go toward this camera.


Best Overall Interchangeable Lens Camera

If you want to get into the interchangeable camera game then you may want to look into the Canon
EOS Rebel T6 DSLR Camera which you can get with a 18-55mm Lens for only $399

These cameras may look similar but they are very different. One way these cameras are different
are the prices. The price on interchangeable lens camera is a side effect of the image
quality compared to point-and-shoot cameras.
